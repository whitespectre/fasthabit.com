Whitespectre Corp. built FastHabit Intermittent Fasting app as a Freemium app. This page is used to inform visitors regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information if anyone decided to use our Service.

If you choose to use our Service, then you agree to the collection and use of information in relation to this policy. The User Information that we collect is used for providing and improving the Service. We will not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection and Use

FastHabit doesn’t collect any user's personal information such as name, address, date of birth, device ID or IP address. We do collect usage information you provide through the Services, such as type of user (PRO vs. FREE), the length of your fasts and reminder settings. We use the collected data for the sole purpose of enhancing our apps. We use the data to make our user experience as friendly as possible. This is the primary way we get usage feedback from our apps.

For a better experience, while using our Support Service via email, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information such as your email address or Ticket order ID. The information that we request will be retained by us and used only for support purposes.

We may employ third-party companies and individuals (i) to facilitate our Service; (ii) to provide the Service on our behalf, (iii) to perform Service-related services or (iv) to assist us in analyzing how our Service is used. Security

We make our best attempt to secure the collected data by only using trusted Apple and iCloud services over a secure connection. We do not store the data ourselves, but we do use Apple’s iCloud storage.

Children’s Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. In the case we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time, so please review it frequently. Changes and clarifications will take effect immediately upon their posting on the website. If our Service is acquired or merged with another company, your information may be transferred to the new owners so that we may continue to sell products to you.
